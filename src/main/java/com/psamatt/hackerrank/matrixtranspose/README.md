## Matrix Transpose

Taking a matrix of numbers, and inline, transpose the numbers
diagonally where x = y

Therefore considering the following examples:

**2 x 2** 
```markdown
1 | 2    ->    1 | 3
3 | 4          2 | 4
```
**3 x 3**
```markdown
1 | 2 | 3          1 | 4 | 7 
4 | 5 | 6    ->    2 | 5 | 8
7 | 8 | 9          3 | 6 | 9
```
